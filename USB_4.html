USB 4

USB 4 (short for Universal Serial Bus 4) is a USB standard released in 2019. It is not a specific line of cable, but instead a standard for the industry, for example Thunderbolt 4 is a USB 4 cable, but not all USB 4 cables are Thunderbolt. All USB 4 cables are USB-C, and USB 4 2.0 allows for up to 120 GBit/s./p>
